How do I manage my studio subscription? expand_more
You can manage your subscription directly from the Settings > Billing section of your dashboard. There you can update payment methods, view past invoices, and change your current plan tier at any time.
Can I invite team members to my workspace? expand_more
Yes, depending on your plan, you can invite multiple collaborators. Go to Workspace Settings > Members and enter their email addresses to send invitations with specific role permissions.
What is your refund policy? expand_more
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new annual subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, please contact our support team within 14 days of your purchase for a full refund.
Do you offer custom enterprise solutions? expand_more
Absolutely. For large studios requiring dedicated support, custom integrations, and unlimited storage, please reach out to our sales team for a tailored quote.
